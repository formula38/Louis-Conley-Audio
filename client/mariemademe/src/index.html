A Passion for Innovative & Immersive Audio Experiences

Louis Conley, a freelance audio engineer based in Sacramento, CA, offers remarkable versatility. Beyond sound design, his proficiency spans Digital Marketing, Cybersecurity, and Software Engineering. With over 15 years of experience and a continuous commitment to learning, Louis brings an unparalleled level of professionalism and expertise to his work.

Adaptability is essential for a thriving audio engineer, and Louis embodies this quality seamlessly. With a background spanning podcast engineering, audio engineering, and freelance recording and mixing, he comprehends the nuances of audio across diverse platforms, audiences, and content genres. Louis has also ventured into the realms of production sound mixing and post-production mixing, broadening his expertise to seamlessly navigate every phase of audio production. From the finesse required for classical concerts to the impactful nuances of dialogue in the film industry, Louis brings a well-rounded understanding to the audio landscape.

Louis is proud to contribute to the audio industry, adding diversity and a fresh perspective. He brings unique insights and skills to every project he undertakes. Louis is known for fostering positive collaboration, ensuring that each project unfolds seamlessly and remains an enjoyable process. His ability to work harmoniously with others makes him an invaluable asset to any team.
